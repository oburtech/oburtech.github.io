Microsoft Office 365 Migration

We’ll work with you to select the migration that best suits your needs to move your data from on prem-applications to the Cloud:

cutover (one extensive process),

batched (planned stages),

hybrid (on-prem disabled after Cloud migration is complete), and

Internet Message Access Protocol (IMAP—transition users from Gmail or other email systems that don’t involve on-prem hardware, e.g. cloud-based systems).
